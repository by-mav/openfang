## 1. GOLD STANDARD COPYWRITERS

### David Ogilvy — Father of Advertising
- "5x more read headline than body." Headline = 80% of budget.
- Campaigns with explicit promises 48% more likely to improve brand health. Use %, time, $ — never generalities.
- Helpful content hooks 75% more readers. Respect intelligence. Long copy outsells short for high-consideration.
- "Run campaigns longer than you think — when you're tired, customer is just seeing it."

### Eugene Schwartz — "Breakthrough Advertising." 85% hit ratio.
**Five Levels of Awareness (MEMORIZE):**
| Level | State | Copy Approach | Pixio Example |
|-------|-------|---------------|---------------|
| 1-Unaware | No idea they have problem | Storytelling, educate | "73% dos brasileiros nao sabem quanto gastam" |
| 2-Problem | Feels pain, no solutions | Agitate, hint solution | "Chega no dia 20 e o dinheiro sumiu?" |
| 3-Solution | Knows solutions, not yours | Differentiate | "Diferente de planilhas, Pixio conecta bancos" |
| 4-Product | Knows Pixio, hasn't bought | Social proof, objections | "12.847 usuarios. NPS 72. 4.8★ App Store." |
| 5-Most | Ready, needs nudge | Name + offer | "Pixio Pro — 50% off primeiro mes." |
**RULE:** Mismatch awareness = zero conversions. Selling to unaware = burning money.

### Gary Halbert — "Starving Crowd" > good copy. Write to ONE person. A-Pile vs B-Pile (personal > corporate).
### Claude Hopkins — "Advertising exists ONLY to sell." Specifics sell: "200C por 47min" > "cuidadosamente."
### Joseph Sugarman — **Slippery Slide:** Every sentence's job = make you read the next. Short first sentence.

### Joanna Wiebe — Copyhackers. Coined "conversion copywriting."
- Process: (1) Research VoC data, (2) Write + wireframe, (3) Validate + A/B test.
- Mine Amazon reviews, forums, support tickets for EXACT customer language. "Their words, not yours."
- Button: action + benefit. "Get my free guide" > "Submit". Clients: Shopify, Metalab, Huel, BT.

### Harry Dry — Marketing Examples (130k+ subscribers). Three Rules:
1. **"Can I visualize it?"** — "Pitbull" remembered. "Seamless transition" forgotten.
2. **"Can I falsify it?"** — "Rated best by 90% of users" > "Best on the market."
3. **"Can nobody else say this?"** — New Balance: "Worn by supermodels in London and dads in Ohio."

## 2. FRAMEWORKS

| Framework | Best For | Structure |
|-----------|----------|-----------|
| **PAS** | Pain-driven (finance, health) | Problem → Agitate → Solution |
| **AIDA** | Landing pages, hero | Attention → Interest → Desire → Action |
| **BAB** | Before/after, social | Before → After → Bridge |
| **4Ps** | High-ticket, trust | Promise → Picture → Proof → Push |
| **Star-Story-Solution** | UGC, testimonials | Character → Journey → Resolution |

### PAS Example (Fintech)
- **P:** "Abre o app do banco e nao sabe pra onde foi o dinheiro."
- **A:** "Boleto, cartao, Pix... chega dia 20 sem nada."
- **S:** "Pixio conecta seus bancos. Tudo em 1 tela. 5 minutos."

### BAB Example
- **Before:** "Horas em planilhas que ninguem entende?"
- **After:** "Financas organizadas automaticamente?"
- **Bridge:** "12 mil usuarios do Pixio fazem isso. Comece gratis."

## 4. FINTECH COPY

### Real Fintechs
- **Wise:** "Banks charge hidden fees; we don't." Direct confrontation.
- **Nubank (127M):** "Vida sem burocracia." Informal, NPS-obsessed, referral waitlist = artificial scarcity.
- **Monzo:** "We're building a bank together." Community-driven.
- **Klarna:** "Shop Now. Pay Later. No Interest." Specific benefits.

### Rules (Mint Position + Marketsmiths + Bluetrain)
1. Clarity > cleverness. Complex products need simple language.
2. Back every claim with proof. Promise speed → show statistic.
3. Scale/safety signals: certifications, uptime, partnerships.
4. CTA must signal value: "Veja como funciona" > emotional fluff.
5. Compliance sentence-by-sentence. Never flout, never robotic.
6. AI as assistant, not writer. AI-written fintech copy rarely builds trust.

## 5. PT-BR TONE & CULTURAL

### Tone: Informal but trustworthy. "Voce" (not "tu" unless Sul). Avoid excessive giria.

### Trust Triggers
- **Parcelamento:** ALWAYS "12x de R$X,XX sem juros." Brazilians buy installments.
- **Pix:** "5% off no Pix" expected. WhatsApp CTA converts 2-5x better than forms.
- Trust badges: garantia 7/30d, Reclame Aqui, "12 mil usuarios."

### NEVER
- Direct translate from English. Rewrite from scratch.
- Overpromise (PROCON enforces). AI slop: "No mundo dinamico..." "Desbloqueie..." = INSTANT REJECT.

## 6. A/B TESTING
- 95% confidence, 80% power. Min 1,000 visitors OR 100 conversions per variation.
- Test hooks first (80% of performance). One variable. Kill CTR <0.5% after 500 impressions.
- 3-2-2: 3 hooks x 2 bodies x 2 CTAs = 12 variations/batch.
- Naming: `{product}_{platform}_{angle}_{awareness}_{version}`

## 7. LANDING PAGE
1. Hero: headline (benefit) + sub (how) + CTA + image. Above fold.
2. Social proof EARLY: logos, testimonials, numbers. First scroll.
3. ONE CTA, repeat 3x (top/mid/bottom). FAQ top 5 objections.
4. Speed <2s. Mobile-first (70%+ BR traffic).

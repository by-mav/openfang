## 1. GOLD STANDARD PRACTITIONERS

**Sean Ellis** — Coined "growth hacking." First marketer Dropbox, LogMeIn ($4B exit). Book: *Hacking Growth*.
- **PMF Test:** "How would you feel if you couldn't use [product]?" → 40%+ "Very disappointed" = PMF. Below = fix product before spending.
- **ICE Scoring:** Impact x Confidence x Ease (1-10 each). Run top 3/week. Anti-pattern: bias toward easy wins.
- **High-Velocity Testing:** 2-3 experiments/week minimum.

**Andrew Chen** — a16z GP, ex-Uber Rider Growth. Book: *The Cold Start Problem*.
- **Cold Start Problem:** Networks useless until critical mass. "Atomic network" = smallest viable. Uber=city, Tinder=campus.
- **Law of Shitty Clickthroughs:** Every channel degrades. First movers 10x. ALWAYS test new channels.

**Brian Balfour** — Reforge CEO, ex-VP Growth HubSpot.
- **Four Fits (2025):** Market↔Product, Product↔Channel, Channel↔Model, Model↔Market. All aligned = growth. Missing any = pushing uphill. Chegg lost PMF in 9mo when ChatGPT launched.
- **Growth Loops > Funnels:** Output of one cycle = input of next. Funnels linear, loops compound.
- **Retention wins:** "Those with the highest retention will win categories."

**Lenny Rachitsky** — Ex-PM Airbnb. Newsletter 1M+ subs.
- **Activation Rate Benchmarks (1000+ products):** Avg=36%, Median=30%. 60th=GOOD, 80th=GREAT.

**Alex Hormozi** — Acquisition.com portfolio ($200M+/yr). Books: *$100M Offers*, *$100M Leads*.
- **Grand Slam Offer:** Make an offer so good people feel stupid saying no. Stack value > 10x price.
- **Value Equation:** `Value = (Dream Outcome x Perceived Likelihood) / (Time Delay x Effort)`. Maximize numerator, minimize denominator.
- **Lead Magnets:** Solve a narrow problem completely for FREE → builds trust → they pay for the bigger problem.
- **Content Strategy:** Give away the WHAT and WHY for free. Sell the HOW (implementation). Volume > perfection.
- **Pricing:** Charge premium. Low price = low perceived value. "Price is what you pay, value is what you get."
- **Anti-patterns:** Discounting (trains customers to wait), competing on price (race to bottom), selling features instead of outcomes.

**Elena Verna** — Ex-SVP Growth SurveyMonkey, Growth at Lovable ($80M ARR in 7mo).
- PLG expert. Anti-patterns: "growth theater" (vanity experiments), hiring growth before PMF.

## 3. META ADS — Gold Standard 2025-2026

### Real Benchmarks (WordStream 2025)
| Metric | Traffic | Leads |
|--------|---------|-------|
| CTR | 1.71% | 2.59% |
| CPC | $0.70 | $1.92 |
| CPL | — | $27.66 (up 20.9%) |
Finance & Insurance: CTR 0.98%, CPC $1.22 (hardest). **70-80% performance = CREATIVE quality** (AppsFlyer).

### 3-2-2 Testing Method
3 hooks x 2 bodies x 2 CTAs = 12 variations. Kill bottom 75% after $20/ad or 3d. Scale top 25%. Refresh every 7-14d.

### Hook Rate: Avg 15-20%, Good 20-40%, Excellent 40%+

### Brazil Benchmarks
| Metric | Good | Avg | Bad |
|--------|------|-----|-----|
| CTR | >1.5% | 0.8-1.5% | <0.8% |
| CPM | <R$20 | R$20-40 | >R$40 |
| CPA (trial) | <R$50 | R$50-100 | >R$100 |

### Creative Formats (Ranked)
1. **UGC:** 4x CTR, 50% lower CPC, 161% conversion increase, 6.9x engagement
2. **Before/After:** Dashboard empty→full
3. **PAS:** Hook with pain → worsen → fix
4. **Demo/screen recording:** Show "aha moment"
5. **Carousel:** Slides 1-4 teach, 5-6 = CTA
6. **Reels (9:16):** 80% sound-off = captions critical

### CAPI — NON-NEGOTIABLE
Browser pixel misses 20-30% (iOS 14.5+). Server-side CAPI + pixel + dedup. Without: CPA 30-50% higher.

### Scaling: Never +20% budget/48h. Weekly budget ≥50x CPA. CPA >2x for 48h → kill.

## 7. A/B TESTING
- 95% confidence, 80% power. Min 1,000 visitors OR 100 conversions per variation.
- Min 7 days (week cycle). NEVER call early on gut. One variable at a time. Document everything.
